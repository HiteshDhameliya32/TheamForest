Avatar

The Avatar component is used to represent a user, and displays the profile picture, initials or fallback icon.

Shapes

Default avatar is square in shape. For rounded and circle avatar add .avatar-rounded modifier classes respectively. 

                    
  <img src="..." class="avatar-lg rounded-circle" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-lg rounded" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-lg rounded-0" alt="">
Sizing

Fancy larger or smaller avatar? Add .avatar-xxl , .avatar-xl , .avatar-lg , .avatar-md , .avatar-sm , or .avatar-xs for additional sizes. 

                    
  <img src="..." class="avatar-xxl rounded-circle" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-xl rounded-circle" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-lg rounded-circle" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-md rounded-circle" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-sm rounded-circle" alt="">
  <img src="..." class="avatar-xs rounded-circle" alt="">
