Shadows

Add or remove shadows to elements with box-shadow utilities.

Shadow

You can also quickly add or remove a shadow with our box-shadow utility classes. Includes support for .shadow-none and three default sizes. 

                        
<div class="shadow-none bg-light rounded">No shadow</div>
<div class="shadow-sm rounded">Small shadow</div>
<div class="shadow rounded">Regular shadow</div>
<div class="shadow-lg rounded">Larger shadow</div>
Shadow Direction

Change the directions of shadow with custom css. 

                      
<div class="shadow-top rounded"> shadow top </div>
<div class="shadow-right rounded"> shadow right </div>
<div class="shadow-bottom rounded"> shadow bottom </div>
<div class="shadow-left rounded"> shadow left </div>
