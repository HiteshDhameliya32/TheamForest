Introduction

Landik is a superb, one of a kind, and present day HTML5 Template having multi-choices for Startups, App Landing, and SaaS-based companies. It has 4 special points of arrival with multi internal pages for different purposes. – Landik is the way to go!

Installation

To setup the landing template, follow below-mentioned steps

  • Visual Studio: Make sure to have Visual Studio downloaded on your machine.
